Taco Dish

Description

This recipe describes how to make classic ground beef tacos with a flavorful, seasoned filling and fresh toppings. It's a simple, customizable meal that is perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • Mexican Chorizo
  • Corn Or Flour Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook the Chorizo until fully browned.
  2. Warm the tortillas on a comal or skillet.
  3. Assemble by filling warm tortillas with cooked chorizo.
  4. Add your desired toppings.

    5. Common Toppings

    • Onion
    • Avocado
    • Queso Fresco
    • Lime Wedges
    • Salsa
